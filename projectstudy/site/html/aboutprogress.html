Art and design for me is a neverending journey. Every project or design is a start of a new adventure. Inspired by different mediums its a continously changing and multifaceted experience. Art and creativity are my passions in life and I love to bring new and creative ideas to each project. I am currently working as the Museum/digital cuator at The German-American Heritage Museum of the USA. As the Museum Curator, I focus on bringing people to the museum as well as creating a fun and interesting atmosphere for all visitors. This includes engaging people on all our social media platforms, curating the museum to make it visually appealing, and creating virtual museum tours.

For digital mediums I work with Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop and Procreate. Other mediums include acrylic paint, charcoals, watercolor, pen and paper.
