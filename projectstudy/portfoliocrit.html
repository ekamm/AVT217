Thai Pham

Thai Pham is a editoral, fashion and wedding photographer. When you enter the page you are welcomed with big image and a clean, miniamalistic layout thath matches her fashion aesthetic. Her page is easy to navigate and allows the viewer to enjoy everything about her page. I would say this is a very basic grid format. She wants the viewer to focus on her photogrpahy.

Steve Wolf

Steve Wolf is a graphic designer and a brand creator. His website is visually stunning with high quality photos. This helps promote himself becuase the images are in a grid layout that help the viewer explore his designs! Scrolling up and down there are a lot of images that the viewer can focus on. each image has a hover navigatino bar over it making it easy to access the images but not have text over the images. Easily readable and overall beautiful.

Studio Bagaz

This website is for a graphic design studio "Bagnaz". Their layout is a fun and sleek aestheitc. Broken up into three parts the website is very minimalistic. Using pastel colors it gives the website a fun and sparky feeling to its very basic structure. It has a simple grid system which helps the viewer get easily move around the website.

Christina Vanessa

Christine vanessa is a graphic designer. Her page is very simple but matches her aesthetic. Similar to Steve Wolf she wants the viewers to focus on her images, and works. THe website is covered by one massive image. With some basic navigation bar and her name simply places over the images. This layout is extremely basic but visually is stunning. She allows for her work to be shown but not confined into small grids or boxes.
